About the Game

Hidden Kitties is a charming hand-drawn hidden object game where players must find cleverly concealed cats across beautifully illustrated scenes. Each level challenges observation skills and attention to detail, rewarding fast players with a star if all cats are found within 15 seconds. With 30 handcrafted levels and 10 hidden cats per stage, the game offers quick, satisfying gameplay sessions that are easy to pick up but hard to master. Its cozy art style and fast-paced challenge create a highly replayable experience that appeals to both casual players and completionists.
